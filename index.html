About Me

I'm a bilingual (English/Spanish) data and business analyst focused on business intelligence and decision support, with 15+ years of professional experience across the U.S. and Spain. I combine a strong foundation in Business Administration (MBA, e-business specialization) with hands-on work in data analytics, business intelligence, and financial analysis.

My career started in Spain in a business services environment, where I supported financial reporting, budgeting, and process improvement using ERP systems and Excel. After relocating to the U.S., I transitioned into education, applying the same analytical and leadership skills to track performance, build dashboards, and translate complex data into clear, actionable insights for students, families, and school leadership.

Today, I'm focused on analytics full-time and currently completing the Data Analytics Accelerator (DAA), a highly hands-on program centered on real-world projects. I work regularly with SQL, Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and Python on real-world datasets to clean data, analyze trends, build dashboards, and answer business questions that support decision-making. I'm actively building a portfolio of data analytics and business intelligence projects.
